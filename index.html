Japan Country

Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its rich culture, advanced technology, and beautiful landscapes. Famous for traditional arts, modern cities, and delicious cuisine like sushi. Iconic landmarks include Mount Fuji and temples.

Jikoshokai (自己紹介)

The word "Jikoshokai" literally means "self introduction" in Japanese. During a Jikoshokai, you might share information like:

  • Your name
  • Your Age
  • Your hobbies or interests
  • Your educational background
How To Do Your Jikoshokai

Start with Greetings!
"Konnichiwa, Minna-San" (Hello, Everyone!)
"Ohayou Gusaimasu, Minna-San" (Good morning, Everyone!)

How to introduce your name:
"Watashi no namae wa __(Your name)___ desu." (My name is______.)

How to introduce your age:
“Watashi wa _____ sai desu." (I am ______ years old.)

How to introduce your educational background:
"Watashi no senkou wa __(your course)___ desu." (My course is______.)

How to introduce your hobby:
"Watashi no shumi wa __(Your hobby)___ desu." (My hobby is______.)

How to say "Nice to meet you":
"Minna-san Yoroshiku Onegai shimasu" (Nice to meet you all)

Bow
After every interaction like Jikoshokai or SelfIntroduction, do not forget to bow as a sign of respect to the Japanese people

How to Count Hours?

"How to count hours" is basically used when telling time in Japan:

  • 1 hour - ichi-ji
  • 2 hours - ni-ji
  • 3 hours - san-ji
  • 4 hours - yo-ji
  • 5 hours - go-ji
  • 6 hours - roku-ji
  • 7 hours - shichi-ji
  • 8 hours - hachi-ji
  • 9 hours - ku-ji
  • 10 hours - juu-ji
